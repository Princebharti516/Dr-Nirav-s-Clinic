Blood Tests

At Mehar Cardiac Clinic, we offer a comprehensive range of blood tests to assess your overall health and diagnose heart conditions. This includes Kidney Function Tests (KFT), Renal Function Tests (RFT), Complete Blood Count (CBC), lipid profiles, blood sugar levels, and other specialized tests to monitor your heart and kidney health. Dr. Nirav Kumar, Head of the Cardiology Department at Big Apollo Spectra Hospital, Patna, ensures that all tests are conducted with precision, using advanced diagnostic tools. These tests help in identifying risk factors, detecting heart disease early, and guiding the right treatment plan. With the expertise of Dr. Nirav and the latest medical technology, we ensure that each patient receives accurate and timely results to manage their health effectively.
