Heart Surgeries

At Mehar Cardiac Clinic, we offer expert consultation for various cardiac surgeries, including Angioplasty, Pacemaker implantation, and Angiography Test. While the initial consultations and assessments are conducted at our clinic, all surgeries are carried out by Dr. Nirav Kumar, who is the Head of the Cardiology Department at Big Apollo Spectra Hospital, Patna.

Dr. Nirav is highly skilled in performing Angioplasty to open blocked coronary arteries, conducting Angiography to assess heart vessel conditions, and implanting Pacemakers for patients with irregular heart rhythms. His vast expertise, combined with the state-of-the-art facilities at Big Apollo Spectra Hospital, ensures that you receive the highest level of care and precision in every procedure.
